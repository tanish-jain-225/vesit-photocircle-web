About VPC

VESIT PhotoCircle (VPC) is a student-run club of photography enthusiasts at Vivekanand Education Society’s Institute of Technology (VESIT) in Chembur, Mumbai. We cover campus events, host training workshops, organize photowalks and contests, and maintain an active social media presence.

What we do

Event Coverage

We cover college fests and events: Prarambh, Sphurti, Utsav, Illusion, convocation, sports meets, hackathons, and more.

Workshops

We conduct sessions on camera basics, composition, editing, and event documentation for members and interested students.

Photowalks & Contests

We host photowalks and run photo/reel contests in collaboration with student councils and clubs.
